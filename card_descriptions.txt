Status Cards (Value 0)Injury (Str): Hand clog . 4th drawn causes instant character death . (Rest or 1 free/reshuffle clears) .  Stress (Int): When drawn, immediately duplicates an identical copy to discard . (Rest/Reshuffle clears) .  Dazed (Dex): Must play if valid . Purged permanently from the game on play . (Rest/Reshuffle clears) .  Curse (Wrd): GM narrative compulsion . Breaking it adds more status . Only cleared by GM conditions .  Blessed (Div): Protected by divine favor . The next status that would enter your hand is discarded instead, then Blessed is removed .  Trick Loss ConsolationsBrace (Str Played): Stage card; add its full value to your next play, regardless of suit .  Reposition (Dex Played): Override automated rule; choose open hand’s next valid play .  Recalculate (Int Played): Discard any 1 card from hand, then draw 1 fresh card .  Revelation (Wrd Played): Choose 1 status card from your discard . Your next played card counts as that status card's suit in addition to its own, then put that status card on top of your deck .  Benediction (Div Played): Cleanse 1 status card from your hand or discard . If none is cleansed, draw 1 card .
