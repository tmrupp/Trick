Player Aid

Trick-Taking RPG Quick Reference

A concise table-use summary of core play, status effects, consolation actions, recovery, and deck evolution.

Core Rules

  • Hand size is 7 cards.
  • Trump is determined by the suit of the current check.
  • The open hand always plays the leftmost valid card and must follow the led suit.
  • Highest trump card wins the trick. If no trump is played, the highest card of any suit wins.

Suits and Status

Suit Domain Status Key Effect
Strength Physical force, endurance Injury Death when the 4th Injury is drawn into hand. Two Injuries in hand at challenge start triggers a survival event.
Dexterity Agility, timing, precision Dazed Must be played if valid. Permanently removed from the game when played.
Intelligence Knowledge, reasoning, recalculation Stress When drawn, places an identical copy into the discard pile.
Weird Magic, the unknown Curse GM-written compulsion. Removed only by fulfilling the GM-set condition.

Trick Loss Consolations

Strength

Brace: Stage the lost card outside your hand. Add its full value to your next played card regardless of suit.

Dexterity

Reposition: Choose which valid card the open hand plays next trick instead of the normal leftmost rule. No effect if only one valid play exists.

Intelligence

Recalculate: Discard any 1 card from your hand, then draw 1 from the top of your deck.

Weird

Revelation: Choose 1 status card from your discard. Your next played card counts as that status card’s suit in addition to its own, put that status card on top of your deck.

Recovery

Rest

Remove any number of status cards from your deck. Curse cannot be removed by rest.

Reshuffle

When your deck empties, remove exactly 1 eligible status card from discard before shuffling. Curse is not eligible.

Deck Evolution

At reshuffle, after your 1 free status removal, trigger evolution for each suit with 3 or more matching status cards in your deck.

Suit Add Remove
Strength Draw 3 Strength, keep 1. Remove your lowest-value non-status card.
Intelligence Draw 3 Intelligence, keep 1. Remove any 1 non-status card.
Dexterity Draw 3 Dexterity, keep 1. Also remove any 1 non-status card.
Weird Draw 3 Weird, keep 1. Destroy 1 non-status card; the GM gives all other players the same Curse.
